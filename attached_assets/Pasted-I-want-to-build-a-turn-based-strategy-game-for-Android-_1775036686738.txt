GAME DESIGN DOCUMENT
Core Concept
A turn-based strategy game on a randomly generated hexagonal grid where players compete against AI to control territory, manage local economies, and eliminate opponents.

The Map & Terrain

Size & Shape: The player selects the exact number of tiles before starting (Min: 40, Max: 200). The board shape should be organic and completely contiguous.

Accessibility: Map generation must ensure all non-mountain tiles are connected.

Terrain Types: Grass (+1 credit), Desert (0 credits), Mountain (impassable, neutral), City (+2 credits, neutral).

City Buffers: Starting Cities must have a 1-tile neutral buffer zone.

Visuals: Thick border outline in player colors around contiguous territories.

Economy & Territory Management

Independent Economies: Each cluster has its own balance.

Splitting: Larger part keeps credits, smaller part gets 0. (50/50 if equal).

Bankruptcy: If balance < 0, all units in that territory die.

Dynamic Cities: Territories of 10+ tiles upgrade the most central non-city tile to a City. Happens only once per territory lineage.

Entities (Units & Buildings)

Units (Move 3 tiles on own land / 1 on enemy land):

Simple Unit (Price 10, Strength 1, Upkeep 1).

Advanced Unit (Price 20, Strength 2, Upkeep 2).

Expert Unit (Price 30, Strength 3, Upkeep 3).

Buildings (Immobile):

Tower (Price 10, Strength 1, Upkeep 1).

Castle (Price 20, Strength 2, Upkeep 2).

Combat & Rules

ZoC: Entities protect their own tile and all adjacent owned tiles.

Combat: Move into ZoC only if Strength > protector Strength.

Rebels: After round 20, tiny chance to spawn stationary Rebels (Strength 0) on owned tiles. They block income and spread.

UI & Information

Fog of War: Enemy credits and affordability flags are hidden.

Affordability Flags: Show a flag on the central tile if a territory has >= 10 credits.

In-Game Bottom Menu: "Back to Menu", "End Turn", "Credit Display", and a "Build" button.

Purchase Ribbon: Clicking "Build" opens a horizontal ribbon above the menu showing all entities. Affordability is checked against the selected tile's territory; unaffordable items are grayed out.

Smart End Turn: "End Turn" button pulses/blinks if no moves/purchases are left.

AI Profiles

1-3 opponents with selectable Difficulty (Easy, Med, Hard) and Personality (Warlike, Balanced, Defensive).
